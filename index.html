Hegemony and Meritocracy in Tech

About the Survey

Welcome to our LIS 500 final project, which includes a survey, results, and analysis on hegemony.

The purpose of this survey is to measure bias regarding hegemony, meritocracy, and gender roles in the technology sector, all topics we learned about and discussed in class.
